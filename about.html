About

At WeatherGard, we work in home improvement. There is no industry so overrun with liars and cheats as ours. That’s why we care about citing sources—we want to get things right, 100% on the up-and-up, right down to the littlest detail. We want to clearly explain what we do with charts, photos, technical prose—whatever it takes to put it all out there.

When it comes to citations, notes, and references, HTML offers zero help. The web is a cesspool of theft and omitted attribution at least in part because html makes doing the right thing such a pain. Nothing exists to ease that pain, so we created labcoat.
